Know Your Rights

Understanding your fundamental rights and legal protections

Fundamental Rights

Right to Life and Liberty

Protection of life and personal liberty under Article 21 of the Constitution.

  • Right to live with dignity
  • Protection against arbitrary arrest
  • Right to fair trial

Right to Equality

Equal protection under the law without discrimination.

  • Equal treatment before law
  • Non-discrimination
  • Equal opportunity

Right to Information

Access to information under the RTI Act, 2005.

  • Request information
  • Transparent governance
  • Public accountability

Legal Rights During Investigation

Rights of the Accused

  • Right to know the grounds of arrest
  • Right to legal representation
  • Right to medical examination
  • Right to fair and speedy trial

Rights of Victims

  • Right to file FIR
  • Right to compensation
  • Right to protection
  • Right to be heard
